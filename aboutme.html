penguin game

this is a preview of the penguin game i created on scratch with some of my friends at intuit

the game is centered around our character, a penguin

the objective is to hop over the water and icebergs as many times as it can

each time you jump over, you will be awarded with one point and once you reach twenty points, the penguin will move faster, increasing the difficulty of the game
